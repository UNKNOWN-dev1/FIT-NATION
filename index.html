WORKOUT BASICS

1. WARM-UP

  • Light exercises to prepare your body.
  • Examples: Jogging, stretching, or bodyweight exercises.

2. REPS (REPETITIONS)

  • One complete movement of an exercise.
  • Example: One push-up is one rep.

3. SETS

  • A group of consecutive reps performed without rest.
  • Example: 10 push-ups = 1 set. Do 3 sets = 30 push-ups.

4. REST PERIOD

  • Time to recover between sets or exercises.
  • Example: 30 seconds to 2 minutes depending on intensity.

5. VOLUME

  • Total work done in a workout.
  • Calculated as: sets √ó reps √ó weight

6. INTENSITY

  • How challenging an exercise is.
  • For weights, it means the load or resistance used.

7. COOL-DOWN

  • Exercises or stretches to relax muscles after working out.
  • Examples: Light stretching or walking.
